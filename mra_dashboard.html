Real-World Credit Dashboard

Analyzing and Tracking Credits for a Local Motorcycle Racing Club

Description

This dashboard was created to assist a local motorcycle racing club that has been struggling with concisely tracking credits. I created a workbook that contains rider information as well as credits issues and used, and provides it in multiple ways to allow the club to easily and quickly input, consolidate, and maintain credits independent of other systems they utilize such as Quickbooks.

This dashboard utilizes VBA, Input Forms, and Macros.
